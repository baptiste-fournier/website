The history of Formula 1

Formula 1 has existed since 1950 and has seen 34 different drivers win a world championship. The most successful drivers in the history of the discipline are Michael Schumacher and Lewis Hamilton with 7 titles. On the manufacturers' side, F1 has seen 15 different manufacturers win titles, including Scuderia Ferrari which holds the record with 16 titles. The title holders are Max Verstappen and his team Red Bull.
